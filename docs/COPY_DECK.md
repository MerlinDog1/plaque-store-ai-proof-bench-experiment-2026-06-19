## FAQs

### Do I see a proof before I pay?

Yes. The proof bench shows your plaque layout before checkout. You can approve it, edit it, regenerate the typography, or request a human artwork check.

### Is the AI proof the final artwork?

The AI proof is the customer-facing production reference for wording, layout, plaque size, material, border, fixings, and backing. For unusual orders or tight layouts, you can request a human check before production.

### Can I change the wording after seeing the proof?

Yes. Edit the wording and regenerate the proof. You should only approve once spelling, dates, punctuation, and layout are correct.

### How long does it take?

The proof is generated in minutes. Once approved, the plaque is made to order and delivered in days. Exact lead time should be confirmed in the live store based on production capacity.

### Does the price include delivery?

Yes. Customer-facing prices should be shown as including UK delivery.

### What if my wording does not fit?

The proof bench will warn when wording is tight or too dense. You can choose a larger plaque, shorten the wording, remove a border, or request a human artwork check.

### Can I upload a logo?

For the MVP, the main flow is text-led. Business and bespoke pages can include a note field for logo or artwork requirements, with human review as the fallback.

### Can I order a custom size?

Yes. Start with the closest product type, enter the custom dimensions, and review the live price and proof. Bespoke sizes should offer human artwork check before production.

### What materials are available?

The primary MVP materials are brushed brass, polished brass, aged brass, orbital brass with matt lacquer, brushed stainless, and mirror stainless.

### Are the plaques suitable outdoors?

Brass and stainless plaques are commonly used outdoors, but the best choice depends on location, exposure, mounting, and desired finish. The product page should recommend stainless for harder-wearing modern outdoor settings and brass for classic engraved plaques.

### Can I add a wood backing?

Yes, where suitable. A wood backing can make a plaque feel more substantial and can help on benches, walls, and presentation plaques. The backing add-on should show its price clearly.

### Can I choose screws or caps?

Yes. The proof should show the selected fixing style and keep the text clear of fixing positions. Decorative caps should be described as flat circular caps, not domed hardware.

### What should I check before approving?

Check every word, date, name, punctuation mark, line break, material, text colour, size, border, fixings, backing, and quantity. The approved proof is the reference for production.
